How to Apply

Simple Admission Process

Enrolling at Skypoint Angels Academy International is easy. Follow these steps to get your child started on their journey.

1

Fill the Registration Form

Complete the online registration form on this page, or call us to pick up a physical form.

2

Submit Required Documents

Bring or upload your child's documents — birth certificate, previous school reports, passport photos.

3

Entrance Assessment

Your child will complete a friendly placement assessment to ensure they enter the right class.

4

Parent/Guardian Meeting

Meet with our principal or admissions team to discuss your child's needs and our offerings.

5

Welcome to the Family!

Complete enrollment formalities, pay fees, and receive your child's welcome pack. They're now a Skypoint Angel!

Required Documents

Completed application/registration form
Birth certificate (original + photocopy)
Previous school report card (last 2 terms)
4 recent passport photographs
Parent/guardian valid ID
Immunization/health record
Transfer letter (if transferring from another school)
